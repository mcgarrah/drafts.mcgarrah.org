About

Hello, my name is Michael McGarrah and I am a technologist at heart. This is my hobby and personal website combination.

Professional Background

I am primarily working in Cloud Architecture for enterprise infrastructure with a mixture of Machine Learning or other domain expertise I have picked up over the years. I’m a jack of all trades and master of some. Part of what makes jobs interesting to me is gathering up domain expertise in lots of areas. I have over 25 years of professional experience in software engineering, system administration, and infrastructure design.

My LinkedIn page covers my professional career. For an insanely detailed version of my resume, you can view the full print version.

Education

I have been fortunate by having very diverse professional experiences. I attended both NC State University for my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and later the Georgia Institute of Technology for my Masters of Science in Computer Science with a focus in Interactive Intelligence the new name for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My research areas included perception systems, natural language processing, and computer vision using Machine Learning. I am currently pursuing an Executive Masters of Business Administration at the University of North Carolina Wilmington to complement my technical expertise with business acumen.

About This Blog

I have been writing about technology since 2004. This blog covers topics I work with professionally and in my homelab, including:

  • Homelab Infrastructure — Proxmox, Ceph, virtualization, and clustering
  • Networking — SFP+, switches, VPN, SDN, and OpenWRT
  • Cloud & DevOps — AWS, containerization, automation
  • Machine Learning — NLP, computer vision, and applied AI
  • Hardware Projects — Dell Wyse 3040, refurbished enterprise equipment

All content is based on my direct, hands-on experience. I write about what I actually build, break, and fix.
